# What are the biggest implications of harness engineering?

- Type: question
- Marker: Final synthesis grill / article implications
- Created: 2026-07-09T01:32:31.000Z

## Summary

The article implies that the harness may be the most practical near-term substrate for recursive self-improvement: the editable runtime around the model can improve capability without changing weights. This shifts attention from prompts alone to workflows, memory, tools, evals, and optimizer code. It also makes evaluation the central bottleneck, because a self-improving harness is only as trustworthy as the feedback signal used to select improvements.
