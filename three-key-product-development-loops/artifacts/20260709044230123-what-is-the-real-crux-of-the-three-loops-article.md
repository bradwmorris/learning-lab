## Summary

The article's crux is that AI agents speed up the inner engineering loop, so the bottleneck moves outward: from writing code, to steering product direction, to learning from real users. Andrew Ng is suggesting that engineers now need to think in nested loops: agentic coding loops for implementation, developer feedback loops for product judgment and context injection, and external feedback loops for market/user reality. The human role remains essential because humans hold context the AI lacks.
